02

Custom AI agents & automation

An agent doesn't just answer — it gets a job done. It plans the steps, uses your tools to do them, and knows when to stop and ask a person. We build each one around your real workflow.

What it means: instead of a person doing a 12-step task, the agent does steps 1–11 and brings you step 12 to approve.

03 · LLM + ASR + TTS

Conversational & voice AI

04

Document & process automation

Reading paperwork and typing it into systems is exactly the kind of work AI is best at. Invoices, forms, receipts, applications — read, extracted, entered and reconciled.

The win: the data entry that eats your afternoons just gets done, with the odd exception flagged for a human glance.

05 · Knowledge

Custom copilots & knowledge

An assistant that actually knows your business — built on your documents, playbooks and history — living inside the tools your team already uses.

What it means: ask "what's our refund policy for wholesale?" or "draft a quote like the one we did for Acme" and get an answer grounded in your stuff, not the open internet.

06

Integration & data engineering

The unglamorous part that makes everything else work: connecting your systems safely, with scoped permissions, and getting your data into shape so AI can act on it reliably.

No rip-and-replace. Your team keeps the tools they like. We just teach the AI to use them too.
