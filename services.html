Kenius — Services

We advise first, then build what fits.

Kenius is a consultancy, not a product. We start by understanding your business and recommending what's actually worth doing — then design and build it as a custom solution.

01
Start here

AI strategy & consulting

Before anyone writes a line of code, we work out where AI helps you — and where it doesn't. You get a clear, costed plan you can act on, with us or without us.

  • Workflow & opportunity mapping
  • Feasibility & ROI estimates
  • A prioritised roadmap
  • Build / buy / don't recommendations
02

Custom AI agents & automation

Bespoke agents that complete whole tasks across your tools — they plan, act, and ask a human before anything risky. Built around your real workflow, not a template.

  • Plans a task before touching anything
  • Scoped access to only the tools you grant
  • Human approval on the consequential steps
03

Conversational & voice AI

Chat and phone assistants trained on your docs and policies — they listen (speech-to-text), speak (text-to-speech), and get things booked, 24/7, in a voice you approve.

  • Book & confirm appointments
  • Answer FAQs & route callers
  • Take messages & call back
04

Document & process automation

Reading paperwork and typing it into systems is exactly what AI is best at. Invoices, forms, receipts — read, extracted, entered and reconciled, with exceptions flagged.

  • Turn a PDF inbox into clean records
  • Match, reconcile and post automatically
  • Flag only the odd ones for a human
05

Custom copilots & knowledge

An assistant grounded in your company's own knowledge — playbooks, history, policies — living inside the tools your team already uses.

  • Answers grounded in your stuff, not the open internet
  • Drafts in your voice, in seconds
06

Integration & data

The unglamorous part that makes everything work: connecting your systems safely, with scoped permissions, and getting your data into shape so AI can act on it.

  • No rip-and-replace — keep the tools you like
  • If it has an API, we can connect it
