05 — Questions
Do you sell a product, or build custom?+
We're a consultancy. We don't resell a one-size tool — we assess what your business needs, build the right solution around your data, and hand it over so you own it.
Where do we start if we're unsure?+
With a short assessment. In a week or two we map your workflows, find the highest-value opportunity, and give you a costed plan — no obligation to build anything afterwards.
Is our data safe?+
Your data stays yours. We don't sell it or use it to train public models; access is scoped to the job, everything is logged, and you can revoke it any time.
What does it cost?+
The assessment and pilots are fixed-fee, so you know the number up front. We prove the value on one use case, then expand.
