The advising-first principle · how every engagement starts

Requests for information.

RFI log
RFI-01Do you sell a product, or build custom?+

Custom, always. We’re a consultancy: we survey your workflow, draw the plan, and build to it — into the tools you already use. No licenses to a platform of ours, no rip-and-replace.

RFI-02Where do we start if we’re not sure AI fits?+

The survey. One to two weeks, fixed fee: we map where the hours go and hand you a costed plan — even if the honest recommendation is “not yet.” The plan is yours either way.

RFI-03Is our data safe?+

Yours stays yours: never sold, never used to train public models, and agents get scoped access to only the systems the job needs — with a human approval step before anything consequential.

RFI-04What does it cost?+

Fixed fee per phase, quoted on the plan — the survey first, then the pilot (typically four to eight weeks). You always know the number before we draw a line.
