Kenius — Work

Find your business — odds are we’ve built it.

We go deep in a handful of industries where AI quietly pays for itself. For each: the work that’s bleeding time, what we’d build, and what changes once it’s live.

/01
Property management

Every tenant handled — even the 11pm “no heat” call.

Tenants don’t message during office hours. Maintenance requests, lease questions and vendor chasing pile up overnight — and the real emergencies hide among the routine.

  • AI leasing assistant & 24/7 tenant support
  • Maintenance triage — routine vs. emergency
  • Vendor coordination & dispatch

Outcome — auto-handles the bulk of inbound, escalates real emergencies in seconds. (illustrative)

/02
Real estate

Reply to every new lead in 30 seconds — not three hours.

Speed-to-lead wins deals, but agents are out showing homes while portal leads go cold. The follow-up that does happen is inconsistent.

  • Lead-qualification agent (budget, timeline, pre-approval)
  • SMS & email follow-up that books showings
  • Listing Q&A assistant

Outcome — sub-minute first response; showings booked to the right agent. (illustrative)

/03
Law firms

Capture the after-hours inquiry — book only the cases worth your time.

Most legal inquiries arrive after hours and go to whoever replies first. Meanwhile attorneys lose hours to intake and document chasing.

  • 24/7 intake & case qualification (jurisdiction, conflicts)
  • Document collection workflows
  • Fees & process FAQ

Outcome — qualified, booked consults; the rest politely declined. (illustrative)

/04
Accounting & CPA

Tax-season document chasing — handled, without one more reminder.

Onboarding and the endless “still waiting on your W-2” chase eat your busiest weeks. Clients mean well; they just need a nudge.

  • Client onboarding & document-chase agents
  • Scheduling & deadline reminders
  • Internal knowledge bot for staff

Outcome — collection runs itself; files land ready to prep. (illustrative)

/05
Home services & trades

Stop sending jobs to voicemail. Answer every call, book it, done.

For HVAC, plumbing and roofing, a missed call is a lost job — and they ring in while you’re on a roof. After-hours calls go straight to a competitor.

  • 24/7 voice agent that books the job
  • Dispatch, confirmations & reminders
  • Quote follow-up

Outcome — every call answered; emergencies routed to the on-call tech. (illustrative)

/06
Freight & logistics

Handle every “where’s my load?” — so dispatch can actually dispatch.

Brokers and carriers lose the day to status calls, carrier check-ins and appointment-setting — while the real exceptions, a detained truck or a missed pickup, hide in the noise.

  • 24/7 track-and-trace & shipment-status agent
  • Carrier check-in calls & appointment scheduling
  • Detention, POD chasing & exception flagging

Outcome — routine status calls handled; dispatch hears only the real exceptions. (illustrative)

Not listed?

Same playbook, your industry.

The pattern — answer, qualify, book, chase, log — repeats almost everywhere: clinics, restaurants, e-commerce, salons & fitness, auto, insurance, recruiting. If your team repeats it every day, there’s an agent for it.
